what is project management?
project management is nothing but planning, scheduling, resource allocation, execution, tracking and delivery of projects.

how the project managers make decision?
before making decision we need to gather the complete information needed to make a decision.

how they take stress?
by planning better and should be positive towards work and our team and mainly should know when to say yes or no

How project managers handle clients?
patience and leadership skills and should not wait for client to know the status we should inform the work status and our plan of action.
